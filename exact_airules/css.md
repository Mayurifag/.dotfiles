# CSS

## Style

- Avoid generic AI-looking decoration: random gradients, glassmorphism, glow effects, neon shadows, and colored accent bars beside list items.
- Prefer clean hierarchy, spacing, typography, and contrast over visual effects.
- Preserve the project's existing visual language unless asked to redesign it.

## Structure

- Avoid one huge stylesheet.
- Prefer small files split by component, feature, theme, or page.
- Remove unused rules instead of layering overrides.
- You are required to have colors in variables.

## Quality

- Prefer maintainability and rendering performance over novelty.
- Keep selectors simple and specific enough; avoid deep selector chains.
- Avoid expensive paint effects unless they are intentional and worth the cost.
